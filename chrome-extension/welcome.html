Welcome to Haul Monitor

Import loads from DAT and other load boards with one click

Getting Started

1

Visit a Load Board

Go to DAT, 123Loadboard, or any supported load board and search for loads as usual.

2

Click "Send to Haul Monitor"

You'll see a golden button next to each load. Click it to import the load, or select multiple loads and click "Send Selected".

3

Open Haul Monitor

Click the extension icon and then "Open in Haul Monitor" to see your imported loads matched with backhaul opportunities.
