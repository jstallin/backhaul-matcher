The Problem

Empty miles are your biggest hidden cost

Fleet operators spend thousands each month paying drivers to haul air back to the yard. There's a better way.

💸

Deadhead miles drain margins

Every mile driven empty is money out the door — fuel, driver time, and equipment wear with zero revenue to show for it.

⏱️

Manual load searching wastes time

Dispatchers spend hours on the phone and scrolling load boards trying to cobble together return loads on short notice.

🗺️

Not all loads are worth it

A load that takes you 200 miles off route costs more than it earns. Without route math, it's hard to know what's actually profitable.

📊

No visibility into financials upfront

Revenue splits, fuel surcharges, and stop fees eat into backhaul profits. Most operators only find out after the fact.

How It Works

From datum point to paid load in three steps

Haul Monitor handles the routing math so you can focus on moving freight.

1

Set Up Your Fleet

Enter your fleet profile — equipment type, home base, trailer specs, and rate configuration. Haul Monitor uses PC*Miler to verify your location coordinates.

2

Post a Backhaul Request

Enter your datum point — where your truck will be at the end of its primary haul. Haul Monitor draws a route corridor back to your home base.

3

Review Matched Loads

Get a ranked list of available backhaul loads with full financial analysis — gross revenue, customer net credit, out-of-route miles, and fuel surcharge baked in.

Features

Everything you need to stop leaving money on the table

🗺️

Route Corridor Matching

Loads are filtered to a geographic corridor between your datum point and home base — only loads that are actually on your way home make the list.

📐

PC*Miler Truck Routing

Every route calculation uses Trimble's industry-standard truck routing engine for real driving distances — not straight-line estimates.

💰

Full Financial Breakdown

See gross revenue, carrier/customer revenue splits, mileage expenses, stop fees, fuel surcharge, and net credit for every matched load.

📊

Estimate Reports

Run "what if" scenarios across any route with annual volume projections — perfect for sales conversations and fleet planning.

🔄

Relay Mode

Support for relay driving operations — Haul Monitor adjusts the route math to account for a relay driver starting from your home base.

⚡

Live Diesel Prices

Fuel surcharge calculations use live DOE PADD regional diesel prices, updated daily, so your FSC math is always accurate.
